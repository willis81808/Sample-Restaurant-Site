To us it's about more than simply food; it's about art.

Come get inspired by the culinary genius of our menu! Our focus is on fresh, local farm-to-table cuisine that combines the best of what the San Francisco Bay Area has to offer. Join us every Saturday morning as we feature a new guest chef, or enjoy our amazing selection of seasonal foods ranging from traditional to modern to exotic. You'll find an ever-changing menu of a la carte dishes, including fresh-made pasta, sandwiches, and salads.

We offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan options. We also have a full bar with cocktails, an impressive wine list, and an extensive wine cellar, with over 30,000 bottles under glass. The upstairs bar includes a fully stocked bar, with a wide variety of beer from all over the world. Come try a few of our famous creations! Take time to enjoy the atmosphere that has been built over the past 14 years in our cozy little kitchen - We look forward to seeing you!
